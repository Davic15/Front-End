For a one-year period from November 2010, the Marugame Genichiro-Inokuma Museum of Contemporany Art (MIMOCA) will host a cycle of four Hiroshi Sugimoto exhibitions

Each will showcase works by the artis thematically contextualized under the heading "Science", "Architecture", "History" and "Religion" so as to present a comprehensive panorama of the artist's oeuvre

Hours: 10:00 - 18:00 (No admittance after 17:30)
